About Pulmonary Disease Prediction

Pulmonary diseases such as Pneumonia, TB, and Bronchitis are major health concerns worldwide. Early diagnosis plays a vital role in preventing complications.

This system combines symptom-based surveys and chest X-ray image analysis to predict the likelihood of pulmonary disease using advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques.

The goal is to provide a reliable, accessible, and user-friendly tool that helps patients and doctors make informed healthcare decisions.
